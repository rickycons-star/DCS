1. Data Producer / Acquisition Vendor onboarding & sourcing (contracts, licensing, entitlements) Producer patterns & data contracts (schemas, SLAs, versioning) Ingestion & connectivity (APIs, feeds, files, streaming) Source data quality validation (at the point of entry, not downstream) 2. Data Governance Ownership & stewardship (Data Owners, Stewards, Custodians) Privacy (PII identification, consent, GDPR/data subject rights, anonymisation/masking) Classification & sensitivity (taxonomy, regulatory tiering, confidentiality labels) Policy management (retention, residency, usage rights, purpose limitation) Regulatory compliance (BCBS 239, DORA, SOX, records management) 3. Data Hydration / Integration Pipeline orchestration (scheduling, dependencies, failure handling) Transformation & enrichment (standardisation, reference data, derivations) Data quality in flight (validation rules, reconciliation, exception handling) Lineage capture (source-to-consumption traceability, automated where possible) 4. Data Storage & Platform Storage architecture (lake, warehouse, operational stores) Environment & infrastructure provisioning (your Auto Provisioning domain) Config management (baselines, drift, change control — your Config domain) Resilience & availability (backup, recovery, RTO/RPO) 5. Data Access & Security Access control (RBAC/ABAC, entitlements, Immuta policy enforcement) Authentication & audit (who accessed what, when, why) Encryption (at rest, in transit, key management) Data sharing agreements (internal cross-LOB, external) 6. Data Consumption & Value Discovery & catalogue (findability, metadata, business glossary) Insights & reporting (dashboards, KPIs — your Insights domain) Data products (published, versioned, SLA-backed assets) AI/ML enablement (feature stores, model-ready data, training data governance) 7. Data Retirement / End of Life (the one most frameworks forget) Retention enforcement (automated deletion at policy expiry) Archival (regulatory hold, legal discovery access) Decommissioning (vendor offboarding, source retirement, downstream impact) Disposal evidence (proof of deletion for regulators)
